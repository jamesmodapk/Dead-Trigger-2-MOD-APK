Dead Trigger 2: Beginner's Guide

"Dead Trigger 2" is a popular first-person shooter gamefirst-person shooter game where players face off against hordes of zombies in a post-apocalyptic world. Starting the game can be overwhelming, but this beginner's guide will provide essential tips to get you on the right path.

Getting Started

Upon launching the game, you'll be introduced to the storyline and basic controls. Pay close attention to the tutorial as it covers fundamental movements, aiming, and shooting mechanics. Utilize the auto-fire feature, which automatically shoots at zombies when you aim at them, making it easier for new players to focus on movement and positioning.

Understanding Resources

Gold and cash are the primary currencies in Dead Trigger 2. Gold can be used to purchase premium items and speed up upgrades, while cash is essential for buying weapons and upgrading facilities. Prioritize completing daily missions and achievements to earn these currencies efficiently.

Tip:

Focus on completing daily missions and achievements to maximize your earnings of gold and cash.

Upgrading Your Base

Your base consists of various facilities, such as the Tech, Medic, Gunsmith, and Engineer. These facilities allow you to craft new weapons, health kits, and other useful items. Focus on upgrading the Tech first, as it unlocks new levels for other facilities and grants access to better equipment.

Weapon Choices

As a beginner, stick with reliable weapons like the MP5 submachine gun and Colt M1911 pistol. These offer a good balance of damage and ammo capacity. Gradually experiment with different weapon types to find what suits your playstyle best. Remember to upgrade your weapons regularly to keep up with increasing difficulty levels.

Utilizing Health Kits and Power-Ups

Health kits are vital for survival. Collect them during missions and use them strategically to avoid death. Additionally, power-ups like grenades and mines can help clear large groups of zombies or tough enemies, giving you an edge in challenging situations.
